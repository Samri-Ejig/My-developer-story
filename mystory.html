My developer story

My name is Samrawit Debebe, i prefere to be called "Samri".

I am a public health professional with 12 years of experience in Mentoring, program coordination, grant and partnership management, Data use, and advocacy for policy change in areas of HIV/AIDS, SRH/FP, quality assurance, immunization, surveillance, and Outbreak Responses. She has strong interpersonal communication, team building, and analytical skill.

After graduating from university, I saved some money to buy my personal computer from my first job. I remember, it was Toshiba, big screen and heavy to travel with. It was the first big thing I bought for myself without any parent support and the excitement was huge.

However, I started to depend on computers and explore more when I joined international NGOs and using Project management softwares, data analytics tools and report writing became a must to acquire skills.

I became more interested in data analytic and visualization tools which led me to take online training on sql, Tableau, Python and PowerBI from Coursera, Udemy and other YouTube Videos.

In addition to my interest and the demand from my work environment , I began to see the world is depending on computers / AI for problem solving and I started to explore more.

When I got a chance to join refcode, I got excited because I believed this would be the beginning for my programming career.
